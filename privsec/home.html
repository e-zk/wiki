Changing $HOME for B and profit

$HOME… always full of… crap.

Worry no more!

#!/bin/sh
# wrap.sh
# usage: wrap.sh <program + args>

BIN=$1
export HOME="${HOME}/opt/$(basename "$BIN")"
[ -d "$HOME" ] || mkdir -p "$HOME"
# run
${BIN}

$ wrap.sh firefox
# opens firefox, but tricks it to think $HOME/opt/firefox/ is the homedir

No more crap in ~.
